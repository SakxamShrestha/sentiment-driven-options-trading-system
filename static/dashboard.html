How TradeSent.AI Works

TradeSent.AI combines real-time financial news with AI sentiment analysis to help you make informed paper trading decisions.

📰 Multi-Source News Ingestion

We aggregate news and social content from Alpaca News, NewsAPI, StockTwits, Yahoo Finance RSS, and Finviz — giving you broad market coverage per ticker.

🤖 AI Sentiment Scoring (FinBERT)

Each news headline and social post is scored using FinBERT — a financial domain BERT model trained on analyst reports and financial news. Scores range from –1 (very bearish) to +1 (very bullish).

📊 Trade Signals

When the average sentiment score crosses a threshold (bullish > 0.6 or bearish < –0.6), the system suggests a BUY or SELL signal. All signals respect the risk circuit breaker.

⚠️ Risk Circuit Breaker

A safety mechanism that pauses all automated signals when extreme market conditions are detected. You can always override manually.

📄 Paper Trading

All trades are executed in Alpaca's paper trading environment — no real money is ever used. Your starting balance is $100,000 virtual USD.

📡 Data Sources

Alpaca News – Real-time financial news WebSocket + REST API
NewsAPI.org – Broad news coverage
StockTwits – Finance-specific social posts
Yahoo Finance RSS – Live ticker headlines
Finviz – Market news and headlines

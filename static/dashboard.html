How TradeSent.AI Works

TradeSent.AI combines real-time financial news with AI sentiment analysis to help you make informed paper trading decisions.

📰 Multi-Source News Ingestion

We aggregate news from Alpaca News, NewsAPI, StockTwits, Yahoo Finance RSS, and Finviz — giving you broad market coverage per ticker.

🤖 AI Sentiment Scoring (FinBERT)

Each article is scored using FinBERT — a financial BERT model. Scores range from –1 (bearish) to +1 (bullish).

📊 Trade Signals

When average sentiment crosses a threshold (bullish > 0.6 or bearish < –0.6), the system suggests a BUY or SELL signal.

⚠️ Risk Circuit Breaker

A safety mechanism that pauses automated signals when extreme market conditions are detected.

📄 Paper Trading

All trades are in Alpaca's paper environment — no real money. Your starting balance is $100,000 virtual USD.

📈 Stock Detail Page

Search any ticker in the top bar to open the stock detail view: live candlestick chart (1m / 5m / 1H / 1D), price, daily change, position, and inline buy/sell form.

🔴 Circuit Breaker

A kill-switch on the Sentiment page. When tripped, the AI pipeline returns HOLD for all signals regardless of score. Click "Trip Breaker" to activate and "Clear" to resume signals.

🤖 FinBERT vs Llama 3

Toggle between two AI models for sentiment scoring. FinBERT is a fast, locally-run financial BERT model. Llama 3 uses the Groq API for large-language-model reasoning (requires GROQ_API_KEY in .env).

🌙 LunarCrush Social Buzz

After running a Sentiment lookup, the Social Buzz card shows Galaxy Score and social volume from LunarCrush — a measure of how much social activity the stock is generating (requires LUNARCRUSH_API_KEY in .env).

🔬 Backtester

Replays historical OHLCV bars for any symbol to simulate what returns a momentum-proxy strategy would have achieved. Set the period, signal threshold, and per-trade notional, then click Run Backtest to see trade-by-trade results.
